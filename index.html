Tilt Buddy

A lightweight mental reset overlay to prevent tilt and keep you locked in.

Tilt Buddy runs quietly in the background while you play. When the game detects a death, it shows a short, practical reset prompt designed to interrupt emotional reactions, slow your tempo, and help you regain focus before tilt starts to build.

What Tilt Buddy does
Tilt usually starts as a physical + emotional reaction — not a thinking problem. Tilt Buddy resets your breathing / posture / focus right when tilt begins: after a death.
  • Detects deaths during gameplay and triggers at the exact moment emotions spike.
  • Shows a short reset prompt (breathing, posture, eye focus, brief pause) and fades fast.
  • No coaching, no analysis, no stats — just a quick mental reset so you stay locked in.
How it works
  • Runs quietly in the background while you play.
  • Death → prompt: a short on-screen cue breaks the tilt loop and re-centers you.
  • Back to the round: lightweight overlay, minimal distraction, disappears quickly.
Who Tilt Buddy is for
Players who tilt after deaths Stop the spiral early — reset before frustration snowballs.
Ranked grinders Long sessions are where small mental slips compound. Keep control.
Competitive players under pressure Focus + composure matter. Tilt Buddy helps you stay stable, not “play better.”
