Stay calm.

Tilt Buddy is a lightweight in-game overlay for Valorant that helps you reset mentally when pressure and frustration start to rise. After deaths or stressful moments, it shows short, practical reset prompts designed to slow things down, interrupt tilt, and bring your focus back to the present round.
