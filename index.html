Tilt Buddy

A lightweight mental reset overlay to prevent tilt and keep you locked in.

Runs quietly in the background. When the game detects a death, it shows a short reset prompt to interrupt emotional reactions, slow your tempo, and bring focus back before tilt builds.

What you get

Triggers on death

Shows a reset right when tilt usually starts — after a death.

Micro-resets

Breathing, posture, eye focus, short pause — nothing long.

No coaching

No “play better” tips. No judgement. No stats. Just a reset.
