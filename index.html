What it does (without the fluff)

Tilt usually starts as a physical + emotional reaction — not a “thinking” problem.

Tilt Buddy gives you a quick, subtle reset at the exact moment tilt usually begins — right after a death. No gameplay advice. No judgment. No analysis. Just a small interruption that helps you stay composed and keep playing clean.

FAQ

Does Tilt Buddy give gameplay advice? +
No. It’s not coaching. It doesn’t tell you what to do in-game — it gives short physical/mental resets to break tilt.
Does it track stats or judge performance? +
No stat tracking, no performance judging. The goal is calm + composure, not analysis.
Is it distracting? +
The overlay is designed to be subtle and short. It appears fast, delivers the cue, and fades quickly so you stay focused.
