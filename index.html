What it does

Tilt usually starts as a physical + emotional reaction — not a “thinking” problem. Tilt Buddy gives you a quick reset right when tilt usually begins: after a death.

  • Detects deaths and triggers at the exact moment emotions spike.
  • Shows a short reset prompt (breathing, posture, eye focus, brief pause) and fades fast.
  • No coaching, no analysis, no stat tracking — just a reset so you stay locked in.
How it works

A gentle interruption at the exact moment tilt begins — without pulling you out of the round.

  • Runs quietly in the background while you play.
  • Death → prompt: a short cue breaks the tilt loop and re-centers you.
  • Lightweight overlay, minimal distraction, disappears quickly.
Who Tilt Buddy is for
Players who tilt after deaths Stop the spiral early — reset before frustration snowballs.
Ranked grinders Long sessions are where small mental slips compound. Keep control.
Competitive players under pressure Focus + composure matter. Stay stable — without “play better” advice.
