Tilt Buddy

A lightweight mental reset overlay to prevent tilt and keep you locked in.

Tilt Buddy runs quietly in the background while you play. When the game detects a death, it shows a short, practical reset prompt designed to interrupt emotional reactions, slow your tempo, and help you regain focus before tilt starts to build.

What it does + how it works

Tilt usually starts as a physical + emotional reaction — not a “thinking” problem. Tilt Buddy gives you a quick, subtle reset right when tilt usually begins: after a death.

The prompts are not gameplay advice. They’re small actions (breathing, posture, eye focus, short pauses) that calm your nervous system and bring attention back to the present moment.

  • Detects deaths during gameplay and triggers at the exact moment emotions spike.
  • Shows a short reset prompt and fades quickly so you stay in the round.
  • No coaching, no analysis, no stats — just a reset so you stay locked in.
