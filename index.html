What Tilt Buddy does

When tilt hits, it usually starts as a physical + emotional reaction (not a “thinking” problem). Tilt Buddy gives you a quick, subtle reset at the exact moment tilt usually begins — right after a death — so you can stay composed and keep playing clean.

It detects deaths during gameplay, shows a short reset prompt, and disappears fast so it never pulls you out of the round. The prompts are simple actions (breathing, posture, eye focus, brief pauses) that help calm your nervous system and bring attention back to the present moment.

Tilt Buddy does not coach, analyze mistakes, track stats, or judge decisions. No gameplay advice — just a mental reset.

How it works

1) Tilt Buddy runs quietly in the background while you play.

2) When you die, it shows a short on-screen reset prompt to interrupt emotional overreactions and re-center.

3) The overlay is lightweight and non-intrusive — it fades quickly so you stay locked in.

Who Tilt Buddy is for
Players who tilt after deaths Stop the spiral early — reset before frustration snowballs.
Ranked grinders Long sessions are where small mental slips compound. Keep control.
Competitive players under pressure Focus + composure matter. Tilt Buddy helps you stay stable, not “play better.”
